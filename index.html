Thuong "tune" Ho

The name is Thuong, but pronounced like "Tune". Perhaps hearing my name over and over has sparked a passion for music in me. Along with being an Above & Beyond groupie, I am also a dysfunctional guitarist. In the very least, if I get stranded on an island with a guitar, I can still jam on some tunes. :)

As the days go by, I find myself enjoying the outdoors more and more. Whenever I can find the time, I try to catch the sun retiring for the day. Something about the sun reeling in its last rays of light, like a sorcerer casting a magical spell over the land and robbing it of light, is quite heartwarming to watch.

Other than that, I am a full-stack developer with an interest in front-end development. At times, I would masquerade as a designer, hoping to attain the "eye" for design.

Why do I code? A question I quite feverishly ask myself while dealing with the challenges that comes as baggage with this madness called coding. Night in and night out, the unsolved mysteries haunt me, slowly eating away at my sanity. Taunting me as I stare it in the eyes. Each pressing moment feels like I am playing a game of Russian Roulette with a loaded gun. Teetering between giving up and continuing, the horror of failing as developer relentlessly plagues my mind.
